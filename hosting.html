Free Internships (Beginner & 1st Semester Friendly)

ISRO Internship & Student Project Trainee Schemes

These schemes offer exposure to space research and technology. While not always AI/ML specific, they provide invaluable experience in a scientific environment.

  • Eligibility: UG/PG/PhD students (Indian citizens) from recognized universities/institutions in Science/Technology. Minimum 60% aggregate or 6.32 CGPA.
  • Duration: Varies (e.g., minimum 45 days for UG, 120 days for PG).
  • Stipend: Generally unpaid.
  • Application Period: Check official ISRO website for specific center announcements. Applications often open periodically throughout the year.
View Official ISRO Page

Emertxe Online Free Internships (Embedded Systems & IoT)

Designed for engineering students, these programs focus on skill-building and project development in core technical domains.

  • Focus: Embedded Systems, Internet of Things (IoT). These build foundational skills relevant to many tech fields, including AI/ML hardware.
  • Duration: Typically 1 Month.
  • Eligibility: Engineering College Students (often suitable for early years).
  • Application Deadlines (Approx. for 2025):
    • Embedded Systems Internship: April 30, 2025
    • IoT Internship: August 2, 2025
Explore Emertxe Internships

NITI Aayog Internship Scheme

A great opportunity to work with a government think tank, gaining exposure to public policy, economics, and governance.

  • Focus: Research, policy analysis, assisting in report drafting across various divisions.
  • Eligibility: Undergraduate/postgraduate students or research scholars enrolled in recognized universities/institutions.
  • Application Period: Online application link is open from the 1st to the 10th of every month.

General Free Internships (Skill India / PW Skills)

Platforms like Skill India Digital and PW Skills list various free internships from different providers, some of which are suitable for beginners.

  • Examples: TutorialsPoint (e.g., Excel-based inventory), Henry Harvin (e.g., Medical Writing, Language Immersion).
  • Suitability: Often focus on practical skills, soft skills, or specific tools. Look for "no prior experience required" or "fresher" tags.
  • How to Find: Browse these portals and filter for "free" and "no experience."

Free Hackathons (Beginner & 1st Semester Friendly)

Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon (ISRO)

ISRO's flagship hackathon focusing on space technology, including AI/ML, image processing, and geospatial domains. Excellent for direct exposure to ISRO's challenges.

  • Focus: Geospatial Domain, Space Science, Image Processing, AI/ML.
  • Eligibility: Undergraduate/Postgraduate/PhD students (teams of 3-4).
  • Event Dates (2025): Grand Finale expected around August 7th & 8th, 2025.
  • Application Link: Check the official ISRO website or Hack2skill for the latest registration portal.
